About one month ago. I started using graphite layout as my main layout with some changes.
Also made this post on reddit: https://www.reddit.com/r/KeyboardLayouts/comments/1oj9rbi/does_anyone_uses_right_thumb_for_kp_in_graphite

pros
- no ph, phy, ky py SFBs
- no stretch for po, ko, or for v

cons
- right thumb overload
- introduces right thumb bigrams/skipgrams like: breakpoint, skip, vape, 
- no single hand ctrl+v
- slower typing?
